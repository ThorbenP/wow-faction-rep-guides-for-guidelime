# Guidelime ThPi - Gnomeregan Rep Farm

In-game reputation farming guide for **Gnomeregan Exiles** in *The Burning
Crusade Classic / TBC Anniversary*, delivered as a **GuideLime** sub-addon.
Open your quest log, pick a zone sub-guide, follow the arrow.

> **Requires [GuideLime](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/guidelime)** as
> a parent addon. This guide registers itself as a sub-guide and shows up
> under GuideLime's faction category in-game.

> **Side**: Alliance only — Horde characters can't gain this reputation.

## What you get

A self-contained guide for **Gnomeregan** that:

- lists **all rep-granting quests** in the Questie database for this faction,
  plus the prerequisite chains needed to unlock them — currently
  **+18965 rep across 17 zone sub-guides**,
- is **split per zone**, with a level range and an in-zone walkthrough,
- shows **map waypoints** (`[G x,y zone]`) for every pickup, objective and
  turn-in,
- separates **complex cross-zone chains** into their own section at the end of
  each zone, so the in-zone path stays clean,
- carries **race / class restrictions** as native GuideLime tags, so the same
  guide works for every applicable Alliance character.

## Installation

1. Make sure **GuideLime** is installed and enabled.
2. Download this guide from the *Files* tab.
3. Extract the `Guidelime_ThPi_GnomereganRepGuide` folder into your
   `World of Warcraft/_classic_/Interface/AddOns/` directory.
4. Launch WoW. The guide appears inside GuideLime under *Gnomeregan Rep Farm*.

## How to use in-game

1. Open GuideLime (`/guidelime`).
2. Pick the *Gnomeregan Rep Farm* guide.
3. Choose a zone sub-guide that matches your level. Each one is annotated
   with its level range and an estimated rep yield, e.g.
   `Dun Morogh (Eff. 1-12, +7410 rep)`.
4. Follow the on-screen waypoints. GuideLime hides steps that don't apply
   to your race / class automatically.

## A note on the guide content

The guide text is produced **programmatically** from the
[Questie](https://github.com/Questie/Questie) database rather than written by
hand. The full generator is open-source on GitHub:

- **Repository**: <https://github.com/ThorbenP/wow-faction-rep-guides-for-guidelime>

Coverage is broad and consistent, but individual quests can occasionally have
an odd waypoint, a missing objective coordinate, or a sub-optimal route. If
you spot something obviously wrong, please open an issue on the GitHub repo
(or on the *Issues* tab here) so it can be fixed in the next release.
